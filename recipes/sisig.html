Sisig

      Pork sisig is a popular Filipino dish. It can be considered as a main dish
      or an appetizer. The composition of this dish is mainly of pigâ€™s parts
      such as minced pork meat, ears, and face. Chicken liver, onions, and chili
      peppers are also added.

Ingredients

For boiling

  • 1 lb. of pig ears
  • 1 lb. of pig snout
  • 1 lb. of pork belly
  • 1 quart of water
  • 2 minced onions
  • 4 thumbs of crushed ginger
  • 2 teaspoon of salt
  • 3 bay leaves

For the dressing

  • ½ cup of Lady's Choice Mayonnaise
  • 2 tablespoons of vinegar
  • ¼ teaspoon of ground black pepper
  • 1 teaspoon of salt
  • 1 teaspoon of sugar
  • ¼ cup of liver spread
  • 2 limes
  • 1 tablespoon of Knorr Liquid Seasoning

Procedures

  1. Put the pig ears, snout, pork belly, bay leaves, 2 teaspoon of salt and 1 quart of water in a cooking pot.
  2. Apply some heat, and boil all of this for 1 hour.
  3. After 1 hour, take the Ingredients out and drain your water
  4. Start heating up the grill
  5. Put the meat you boiled earlier on the grill and cook for 5 minutes
  6. After 5 minutes, turn the meats to the other side
  7. Repeat the previous steps for 20 minutes
  8. Transfer the cooked pig parts to a separate container and let it cool down
  9. Combine all the dressing ingredients in a bowl and mix them thoroughly
  10. Chop up the pig parts you grilled earlier into small pieces
  11. Place the chopped meat and dressing in a large mixing bowl.
  12. Start mixing the meat and dressing until they are well-blended
  13. Prepare the sizzling plate on the griller to pre heat them
  14. Add butter on the sizzling plate and dissolve it
  15. Once the butter is dissolved, you can then add the mixture of meat and dressing to the sizzling plate
  16. Cook it for 5 minutes while mixing
  17. After 5 minutes you can now serve it
